Student Loan Coin (SLC)

About the Project

Student Loan Coin (SLC) is a social impact token born from debt. The system is broken — institutions borrow at 1-2% and lend to students at 6-8%, locking an entire generation into financial servitude.

We’re building a community-powered, decentralized system that pushes back. Every transaction fuels our mission:

  • 1% to Forgiveness Wallet 🏦
  • 1% to Marketing 📢
  • 1% to Liquidity 💧
  • 1% to Operations ⚙️

This isn’t just a token — it’s a movement.

Vision Flow

  1. Launch
  2. Buy $SLC
  3. Price rises with demand
  4. 1% of each transaction funds Forgiveness Wallet
  5. Forgiveness applications open
